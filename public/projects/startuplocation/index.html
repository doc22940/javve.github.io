Said about StartupLocation

A few months back we made a map over hot startups located in ourcity, Stockholm. We began with startups we knew, but got reallythrilled over finding so many new ones. The map got retweeted a lot. Wealso made a twitter list with Stockholm startups that now has 85members. Stockholm is really cooking. Since many people thought thiswas a great initiative, we decided to take it one step further - startuplocation was born.

Our aim with Startuplocation is that it will be a way for you tokeep track of what’s happening in the startup scene in your city. Andin the future, hopefully a way to keep track of what’s happening inthe startup scene overall.

Twitter: StartupLocation

The reason is simple - the scene is changing rapidly and thereshould be a better way to keep track of it.
